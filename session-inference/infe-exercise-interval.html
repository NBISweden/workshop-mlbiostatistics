Exercises: Interval estimates

The exercise Rmd can be downloaded from the inference exercise files directory, also solutions (both Rmd and html) can be downloaded from the same directory (but with a time restriction).

Exercise 8 You measure the Hb value in 10 50-year old men and get the following observations; 145, 165, 134, 167, 158, 176, 156, 189, 143, 123 g/L.

  1. Compute a 95% bootstrap interval for the mean Hb value.
  2. Compute the sample mean Hb value
  3. Compute the sample variance
  4. Compute the sample standard deviation
  5. Assume that Hb is normally distributed and compute a point estimate and a 95% confidence interval for the mean Hb value.
